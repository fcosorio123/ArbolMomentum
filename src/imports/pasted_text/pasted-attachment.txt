Main Goal: Lose 20 pounds this summer (by end of August/early September) while building sustainable strength, energy, neck resilience, and life balance.
Total Timeline: 16–20 weeks (4–5 months)
Approach: Progressive, realistic, and sustainable. 1–1.5 lb average loss per week.

Weekly Training Schedule (Progressive)
Phase 1 (Weeks 1–4) – Foundation
Focus: Form, consistency, recovery.
Monday: Strength Session 1 (45–55 min) + Walk
Tuesday: Easy Run (20–25 min) + Mental Project (30 min)
Wednesday: Recovery + Wife/Home Night (Mobility 10–15 min)
Thursday: Strength Session 2 + Drums (20–30 min)
Friday: Easy Run or Long Walk (25–40 min) + Comedy
Saturday: Tennis + Social
Sunday: Long Walk (40–60 min) + Weekly Review + Meal Prep
Strength Sessions (2x/week): Full body, moderate weights
Bench Press (balanced with rows/pulls)
Squats or Goblet Squats
Hip Hinge (Romanian Deadlift or Good Mornings)
Core (Planks, Dead Bugs)
Shoulder & Neck mobility finishers

Phase 2 (Weeks 5–8) – Intensification
Increase volume and intensity slightly.
Add 1 more run (now 2 runs/week)
Strength sessions: Add 1 set per exercise or 5–10% more weight if form is good
Walking: Aim for 8,000–10,000 steps daily
Introduce 1 optional HIIT-style finisher once per week (short, neck-safe)
Nutrition: Tighten slightly (reduce late-night eating further)

Phase 3 (Weeks 9–12) – Peak Fat Loss
Strength: Move to 3–4 sets per exercise
Cardio: 2–3 runs/week (mix easy + 1 slightly longer)
Tennis: Keep as fun + cardio
Add 1 weekly conditioning circuit (bodyweight or light)
Weekly Check-in: Adjust calories if weight loss stalls (see Nutrition below)

Phase 4 (Weeks 13–20) – Refine & Sustain
Maintain strength 2–3x/week
Focus more on performance metrics (energy, strength numbers, mood)
Reassess neck pain – consider PT if needed
Transition toward maintenance calories if 20 lb goal is reached
Emphasize enjoyment and flexibility

Nutrition Framework (All Phases)
Main Rules:
Protein priority (target 160–200g/day depending on body weight)
Vegetables/fruit at 2 meals
Calorie deficit: Moderate (aim 500–700 kcal below maintenance)
Flexible on weekends but no full cheat days
Progression:
Phase 1: Focus on consistency, not perfection
Phase 2–3: Reduce ultra-processed foods and alcohol
Phase 4: Find sustainable maintenance level
Advisor Note Needed: Exact calorie/protein target once current weight is known.
